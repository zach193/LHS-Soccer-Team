Mid-Fielders

Mid-Fielders are positioned in the middle of the field between the Strikers and the Defenders. Mid-Fielders are to link the Strikers and Defenders and they often are on both offense and defense.

Defenders

Defenders are the players closest to the net and Goalkeeper. Defenders are responsible for defending the Goalkeeper, blocking shots and stopping the other team's offensive players from passing, receiving, shooting and scoring.

GoalKeepers

The Goalkeeper position is the most specialized position in soccer. A Goalkeepers main responsibility is to prevent the opposing team from scoring. This is also the only position where using your hands are allowed.
